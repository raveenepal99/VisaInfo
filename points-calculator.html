Points are awarded based on your English language test scores. Higher proficiency levels earn more points.

English Proficiency Required Score Number of Points
SUPERIOR ENGLISH a Score of at least 8 in each of the four test components of speaking, reading, writing and listening 20
PROFICIENT ENGLISH a Score of at least 7 in each of the four test components of speaking, reading, writing and listening 10
COMPETENT ENGLISH a Score of at least 6 in each of the four test components of speaking, reading, writing and listening 0

The Test must have been conducted in the 3 years immediately before the day on which the person was invited to apply for the visa and the person achieved the specified score
